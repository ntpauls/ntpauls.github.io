Junior Year: Ecuador Semester - Spring 2020


(L-R) Throwing on my host parents' kickwheel, handbuilding in my parents' at home studio, my host mom handbuilding a figurine based off of the traditional indigenous people of Ecuador.


During the second semester of my junior year, I studied abroad in Ecuador. I was part of a group of students doing Goshen College's 12 week Study Service Term (SST). For the first 6 weeks, my group studied spanish and Ecuadorian culture, taking tours of the capital city of Quito and living with host families.

The second 6 weeks, our group was split up and sent to different service location throughout the country. I was fortunate enough to be placed with a family ceramic business that makes miniture figurines based off of the traditional indigenous people of Ecuador. I spent jusst 3 weeks with the family after my time there was cut short when my group had to leave the country early due to COVID-19.
