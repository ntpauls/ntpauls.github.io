Raleway

&

Raleway was originally designed with a single thin weight by Matt McInerney. In 2012, the font was expanded into a 9 weight family by Pablo Impallari and Rodrigo Fuenzalida (Raleway) . It is inspired by more geometric sans-serif typefaces and is a stylistic alternate to default text face (McInerney). In 2016 the design was thoroughly updated with new spacing added, a raised f bar, italics among with other features added.
