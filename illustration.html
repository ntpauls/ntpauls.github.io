Illustration

I've loved drawing since I was a kid, always having a sketch book with me. Below is the best of my recent illustration work.


In Search of Presence - April 2021


In December 2020, my friend Mackenzie approaced me to illustrate her book "In Search of Presence." The book highlights stories from her time in China and other short stories. Above is a sample of my best work from over ten sketches I did.


Spectrum of Light - February 2021


This series is from my Drawing II class at Goshen College. Each one is of my 6 housemates (including myself). I used pen and marker. Surrounding each person is 10 objects that they named to describe themselves.
