about


Greetings,


Hey! I'm Nathan Thomas Pauls. I grew up in the midst of rolling hills and Amish farms in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. I've loved art and creating things ever since I was young, always keeping a doodle book and working on lego stop-animations in my bedroom.

In high school, I earned a spot on the staff of Millstream, the student newspaper. In the span of 4 years, went from a freshman cartoonist to a senior Features page editor.

​

I continued my enthusiasm for writing at Goshen CollegeI've been a member of the college's The Record for all of my three years. Starting my fourth year I will be Managing Editor for Digital Content. I've also been involved at the college's radio station 91.1 The Globe and this coming year I'll be second in command as Program Director.

​

Goshen helped me find my main passion, pottery. I love throwing on the wheel. Creating with clay gives me a sense of accomplishment, focus, and peace that I can't find anywhere else. Although I don't expect it to be my main line of work (at least not right away) I want to continue working with clay after college.

​

That makes up a short life history, so here are some other fun facts about me:

​

  • I spent a semester studying and serving in Ecuador in the spring of 2020, but my group's time was cut short due to the COVID-19 pandemic
  • I love drawing and the PILOT G-2 05 pen is my tool of choice.
  • Tea is my favorite hot drink, which pairs well with my mug making ability.
    • I enjoy playing video games that go along with my creative spirit, Minecraft Stardew Valley.
  • Music is one of my favorite things. With 62,312 minutes listened on Spotify alone in 2020, I listen to music almost everywhere I go.
